WHY DIALOGUE?

The world needs to teach its future generations how to engage with differences.

Safe spaces to listen, learn, and grow will equip youth to hold multiple truths and perspectives simultaneously.

Youth equipped with the ability to engage in dialogue would end cycles of intolerance and violence.
